A joystick for simultaneously modifying four modulation outputs.
↖↗↘↙
The four modulation outputs, corresponding to (and highest in) the various corners of the joystick.
Show
Reveals the joystick, plus the option to record.
Record
A button for recording the a joystick movement trajectory, which is then played back whenever a note is played. This button has four states:
Record
Arms the recording. The joystick turns green. Drag the joystick to begin recording.
Cancel
Disarms and cancels the recording. The joystick turns blue.
Recording
Indicates that recording is taking place.
Clear
Erases the last recording
Note that when you are moving the joystick manually, all voices are affected. When the joystick is controlled by a prerecording, it affects each note independently and starts when the note is pressed.
